Time Travel, Inc. offers 3-hour trips to any part of the world and at any time the client wishes, each time machine includes 7 seats of which only 4 are available to clients. All travelers must follow the instructions and rules provided by the guide or guard, it must also be announced that anyone who commits an offense will pay a fine starting at $ 10,000.
