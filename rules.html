Rules & Safety

Risks

~By stepping on the machine the traveller agrees that there’s a chance he/she might not get back alive.
~Breaking a rule will cost money.
~Taking off the body suit can cause sickness on the traveller/s.
~Travellers might get dizzy during the trip.
~Passengers might get lost in time.

Rules

~Travellers must obey orders given by the guide or guards.
~Travellers are not allowed to get out of the path.
~Travellers must keep their seatbelts ON when the machine is moving.

Safety

~If the traveller/s feel fever or runny nose please call the office and they must not show up to the trip, our staff will reschedule their trip to a different date.
~Travellers must keep the body suit on at all times. It is made for the prevention of introducing bacterias from this time into another time.
~Travellers should not eat outside the travel machine.
~All passengers should always have guns locked until there’s an order given from our staff members to unlock them.
~Travellers must not open the doors of the travel machine.
~Travellers are not allowed to get out of the path.
~Travellers must keep their seatbelt ON, when the machine is moving.
~Travellers must collect their bullets after shooting, and wear gloves for it.
